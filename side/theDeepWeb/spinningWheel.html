Spinning Wheel

Spinning Wheel, by Blood, Sweat, and Tears holds a very special place in my heart. It was one of the first songs I can distinctly remember my father showing me as a kid, and is probably part of the reason I started playing the trumpet.

To say Merry Christmas and thank him for being so incredible, I did my best to create my own version of Spinning wheel for my dad.

The entire thing was created alone in my bedroom, which made for some interesting replacements to some iconic parts, but I like to think the personal touch is what makes the cover special.
