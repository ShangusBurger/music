Walking

I moved to California in the middle of the pandemic. Often, I found myself deep in my thoughts, and would explore them on long walks through the nearby streets. After so many walks, I found a calming repetition in the sounds of my environment as they would change from space to space. First my bedroom, then my living room, then the hallway of my apartment, then the outside world, then a side street, then a larger street.

The consistency of these environments led me to follow a very similar mental journey whenever I would go walking. So I channeled that journey into a piece of music that accompanies the sounds of those environments.
