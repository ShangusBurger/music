PLAYLAB 2020

Playlab is a winter session class at CalArts, in which students from multiple schools meet for 2 weeks and create a performance from scratch. Two weeks is not very much time to create an entire performance, but somehow we put together something amazing.

There's a bit of mystery around the performance so I won't spoil anything, but with the help of some amazing dancers, graphic designers, musicians, and other creatives, I coded together a webpage to present our performance (and also played a bit of trumpet).
