Fever Dreamin'

Premiered as the closer to my 2021 CalArts Mid-Residence Recital, Fever Dreamin' is a piece for trumpet quartet. It was composed and recorded by myself in an attempt to poke fun at how crazy we've become in our complete social isolation.

It's sporadic, confusing, and oddly familiar at times, much like a horrible terrible dream we're not allowed out of.
