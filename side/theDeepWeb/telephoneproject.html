The Telephone Project

I created this project in the summer of 2019 in an effort to give myself and other artists inspiration to create something new.

The idea was that each creator would take what was sent to them as inspiration for a work of their own, which they would then pass on to the next artist. It began with a painting I made over the course of a few days and sent to a musician friend, and from there, a few more people have added their work.

While the process is slow in its nature, the result is an evolving lineage of creativity and thought.
