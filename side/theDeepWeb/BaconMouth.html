BaconMouth

BaconMouth explores our conception of the face, mouth, and head. It is inspired by the visual art of painter Francis Bacon, who is known for his paintings that distort the human face to make them eerily inhuman and barely recognisable.

This piece took place as part of a larger discussion with fellow artists around the topic of post-humanism, and our collective understanding of what it means to be human, to be less than human, or to be beyond what is human.
