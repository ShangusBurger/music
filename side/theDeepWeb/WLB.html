Winters Last Bones

Directed and Animated by the amazingly dedicated Izabella Anguiano-Jacobs, with original music by Drew Sensue-Weinstein, Winters Last Bones tells the story of a starving witch who hunts down her next unsuspecting meal. The audio-scape is riddled with creepy, sinister, and gross effects, and through close collaboration with Drew, often the music and sound design merge and interact.
