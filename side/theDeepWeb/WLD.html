Weird Looking Dogs

Co-directed by the amazing Young Kang and Anya Butler, with music by the stupendous Roy Berardo, Weird Looking Dogs follows the story of two friends catching up while exploring a dungeon, coming across a few adventurous monsters on their way.

With 10:22 of action-packed scenes, this project was a lot to take on as a solo sound designer, foley artist, and mixer, especially because of its lively cartoony feeling with which I have less experience. But, it has become a project which I am extremely proud of, and will go down as one of the hardest projects I completed in my collegiate years. It also holds a special place in my heart because I got to create the voice of some of the most essential characters to the story, the monsters themselves.
