is this music?

The idea for 'is this music?' began when I asked myself the question, "What is it that qualifies as music?" I noticed that a great deal of avant-garde music would probably exist far outside the definition of music for anyone not living in the contemporary music world. So I started composing a piece to explore the question.

I began with a somewhat standard, boring, inconsequential solo trumpet melody, and slowly started removing the things that made it 'musical.' First the diatonic nature of the melody, then pitch center, then normal rhythm, then recognisable timbre, then instrumental sound, then sound altogether.

The end result is a piece that moves from comfortable and recognisable to uncomfortable and unfamiliar.
