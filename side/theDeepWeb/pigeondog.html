pigeon.dog

pigeon.dog, for the first few months of its existence, was known as the Post-Quarentine Bucket List. It was essentially a google doc upon which a few friends and I would apply our 2am stream of conciousness to help deal with the pandemic.

The smartest and most tech savvy of my friends, David Rademacher, had the brilliant idea of turning this document into a website, so that we could practice our coding, add some features that a google document wouldn't allow, and share it with the world.

It is a constantly growing playground of odd, extraordinarily detailed messages and activities that I constantly get lost in.
