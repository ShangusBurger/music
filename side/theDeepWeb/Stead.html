Stead

Stead is an original found-footage film exploring topics like suburbia, family, and marriage. The sound for the film was created from scratch before any work was done on the picture, which made for a fun and unique creative project.

Stead was one of my first experiments with more experimental sound for film, and one of the first live action films I have been a part of producing. The film premiered at my Graduation Recital: Hoj Poj.

All footage used was taken from the Prelinger Archive of Home Movies.
