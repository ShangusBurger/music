Jesus autem transiens.
Credo in Deum.

This piece was written by Robert Wilkinson in the late 1400s for 13 voices. Adapted for trumpet, it made for a splendid opener to my 2021 Recital, Alone Time.

Recorded with no metronome, 13 layers of trumpet were individually recorded over one another until a completely organic performance emerged.

The video for this project was taken outside my apartment during a particularly nice sunset that I couldn't let go.
