An Odd Dream to Have

This piece was performed by the amazing Erika Duke-Kirkpatrick and mixed by the amazing John Baffa. It loosely follows the plot of a dream I had where the constantly changing surroundings made for a confusing yet beautiful experience. My goal was to make a beautiful piece that was not too demanding of a cellist, and to explore the nuance of solo cello in all its glory.
