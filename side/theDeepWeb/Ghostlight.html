Blue Devils 2019: Ghostlight

Back in 2019, I had the immense priveledge of participating in the Drum Corps International summer tour with one of the most respected organizations in the marching band world, the Concord Blue Devils.

Participating meant living with the corps for 3 months. First in Concord, then on busses and gym floors as we traversed the country, rehearsing, competing, and performing for thousands of fans.

To end an amazing summer, we went on to take 1st place at the DCI World Championships in Indianapolis.

The video on the right is a compilation of two performances, one taken the day before finals during our rehearsal runthrough, and one taken during the 'victory lap' show on finals night.
